Terms of Service

1. Service Agreement

By booking a cleaning service with Maid‑Right, you agree to these terms of service. We provide professional cleaning services for domestic, commercial, and specialist cleaning needs in Melksham and surrounding areas.

Booking: All bookings are confirmed via email or Facebook message. We require at least 24 hours' notice for standard bookings.

Cancellation: Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled service. Late cancellations may incur a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the service cost.

Rescheduling: Services can be rescheduled with at least 24 hours' notice at no charge.

Quotes: All quotes are estimates based on the information provided. Final pricing may vary depending on the actual condition and requirements of the property.

You are responsible for providing access to the property. If keys are provided, we will keep them secure and return them upon completion unless ongoing service is arranged. We are not responsible for any loss or damage if keys are lost or stolen outside of our possession.

Pre-existing Damage: Please inform us of any pre-existing damage or delicate items before the service begins. We are not responsible for damage to items not disclosed or for wear and tear on extremely worn or damaged surfaces.

Pets: Please inform us of any pets on the property. We reserve the right to refuse or reschedule service if we feel unsafe due to animal behavior.

6. Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind our work. If you are not satisfied with any aspect of our service, please contact us within 24 hours at enquiries@maid-right.co.uk. We will work with you to make it right, including returning to re-clean specific areas if necessary.

Maid‑Right carries public liability insurance. We take every precaution to prevent damage or loss, but we are not liable for:

We collect the following information when you book our services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address

  • Payment Information: Billing details (processed securely, we do not store payment card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

We take data security seriously:
